The thinking behind my work

Every solution starts with understanding the business challenge and the people solving it. The technology changes from project to project, but the goal stays the same: create something that helps teams make better decisions and achieve measurable outcomes.

Start With the Business Question

Simplicity Scales

AI Extends People, Not Judgment

AI should take the repetitive work off people's plates and surface what matters, so judgment stays where it belongs: with people.
